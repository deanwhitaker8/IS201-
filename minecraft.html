Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these steps to begin your adventure in Minecraft:

  1. Start by gathering resources. Here’s how you can do that:

    • Chop down trees to get wood
    • Mine stone to make tools
    • Collect food to keep yourself from starving

  2. Next, build your first shelter to survive your first night:

    • Use the wood and stone to build a simple house
    • Place a crafting table inside
    • Make a furnace to cook food
  3. Explore the world to find new materials and biomes.
